Wall & floor tile buying guide

Advice on choosing the right material & size of tile

Whether you're renovating a bathroom, kitchen or hallway or simply adding a bit of extra detailing to a room in your home, Homebase's extensive and exciting range of wall and floor tiles are the perfect materials for the job. Available in a wide range of sizes, styles and finishes, we're guaranteed to have a design to suit both your taste and your home, allowing you to create your perfect interior quickly, affordably and easily.

If you're planning to cover an entire surface with wall tiles or use them in conjunction with wallpaper or paint, we've got the tiles and the tools that you need to complete the job. From tile adhesive and grout to cutters and spacers, here at Homebase we've got the supplies and the knowledge to help you achieve the result that you're looking for. As well as wall tiles, we also stock a wide range of vinyl floor tiles in a variety of styles. These are perfect for use in busy homes as their tough and durable surfaces can withstand almost anything your family can throw at them!

For a unique and stylish finish, have a look at our selection of mosaic tiles. Available in a range of colours, mosaic tiles are perfect for use in both bathrooms and kitchens. To find out more about our range of tiling and accessories or other products in our paint and decorating supplies section, browse the Homebase website today or drop into your local store.
