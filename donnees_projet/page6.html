John H. Lowenstein

Professor Emeritus of Physics

Ph.D. 1966 (physics), M.S. 1963 (physics), Illinois (Urbana); B.A. 1962 (mathematics), Harvard.

Email: john.lowenstein@nyu.edu

Area of Interests: Nonlinear dynamics and chaos, aperiodic long-range order, quantum integrable systems, renormalization theory, quantum field theory.

Office Address: 4 Washington Place, New York, NY 10003

Phone: 212-998-7740

Fax: 212-995-4016
