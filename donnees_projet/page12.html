UK Pro Tiling Training

We are the No 1 and only professional dedicated tiling training centre in the UK, we specialise and only deliver professional fast track Wall and Floor tiling courses at the highest level possible. We have a fantastic purpose built tiling training centre for you to train in and welcome students from all over the UK.

Expert Tutors 

All our tutors are qualified level 3 expert tilers and our tiling units are specially designed to give you real life situations to practice in. We are here to pass on all our trade tiling knowledge to you and give you the best start possible in your new career or the ability to tackle your own personal wall and floor tiling projects in the quickest possible time.

Earnings 

Good quality tilers can earn up to £50k per year once established, some of our past students are now making much more running their companies having gone beyond being a sole tile fixer, let us show you how maximise your earning potential as a tiler with our self employed tiling programs. No other training centre provides the in depth business knowledge and start up programs like we do. Please check out our FAQ at the top menu bar to see how we differ from the rest and why so many people travel from all over the UK and Europe to train with us.

Qualifications

Our fast track courses are designed specifically so you can go straight out and work as a tiler once you have completed the course. If you require an NVQ to go on a building site, we can put you in touch with assessors in your area to gain this work based on site qualification.

Free Aftercare

After leaving a course you may feel that you are alone to get on with it which can be daunting, Not with UK Pro Tiling Training, as well as the 10,000 word, 50 page pre written hand out course notes to refer to, you will have our full aftercare support, not sure of a job coming up, just let us know and we will give you all the reassurance and back up you need, even on the evenings after hours. No other training centre offers this level of personal service. Our focus is to help you succeed in your new career.

Company Training
